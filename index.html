1. Each dice roll gives limited Movements (used to move) and Actions (used to attack)
2. Select a piece by clicking on it
- Deselect by clicking on it a 2nd time
3. Move with [arrow keys]
4. When they are in range, target an opponents piece by clicking on it
- Press [E] to attack
- Deselect by clicking away
5. Press the colored button to [End Turn]

[Click] to start game

Blue Abilities
- Strategization - each white Planner piece that is still on the board gives 1 extra Action per turn
- Hyperdrive - when the dice roll is odd, gain 1 extra Move
Red Abilities
- Chain Reaction - when an attack is successful, no Actions are used
- Scouting - gain 3 extra Moves per turn when neither side has attacked
